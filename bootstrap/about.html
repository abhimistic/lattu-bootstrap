About Us

We are a reputable manufacturing and wholesale firm specializing in the production of a wide range of knitwear products. With our in-house production capabilities, we handle every step of the manufacturing process, ensuring high-quality results. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we prioritize quality and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to excellence is further reinforced by our IEC certification. From the initial yarn selection to the meticulous creation of the final garment, our dedicated team of experienced professionals oversees every aspect of production within our factory. With over 30 years of experience in the apparel industry, we take great pride in our brand, Lattu. Our garments, bearing the Lattu brand name, are known for their fine quality and sleek designs for/under womenswear, menswear and childrenswear. Whether you're looking for cozy cardigans, fashionable sweaters, or versatile knitwear options, we have something to suit every taste and preference.

Our Journey

Our journey in the manufacturing industry began at a young age with a shared vision to bring about positive change in the market. It all started in 1988 when we established a small one-room unit on the ground floor of our house. Over the years, our dedication and hard work have allowed us to expand, and today we proudly operate a factory that accommodates over 500 workers across three different units in the industrial hub of Punjab, Ludhiana. Our humble beginnings involved operating a hand flat knitting machine in the comfort of our home, with just one room serving as both production space and a sales counter. As demand grew, we gradually introduced circular machines and transitioned to a dedicated industrial unit in Ludhiana's industrial area. We are proud to supply our products throughout India, reaching customers far and wide. Our unwavering commitment lies in crafting high-quality garments that offer long-lasting durability to our valued consumers. We believe in delivering products that not only meet but exceed expectations, ensuring that our customers enjoy garments that stand the test of time.
